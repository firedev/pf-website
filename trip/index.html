🏝️ Surin islands

Crystal-clear waters, vibrant coral reefs, and diverse marine life. We stay in tents on a peaceful beach surrounded by an abundance of marine life. Two ocean trips a day with a break in between. Included: — A unique long-tail boat for snorkeling and freediving excursions — Comfortable beachside tents with air beds, mini fans, and lights — All meals, snacks, and drinks — Professional photos and video documentation
